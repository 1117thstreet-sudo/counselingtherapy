Couple / Marriage

Couple and / or marriage counseling provides an arena where two people can learn to communicate individual needs and create a healthy relationship that grows and lasts.

We help couples address issues with:

  • Communication
  • Intimacy
  • Financial
  • Career
  • Parenting Differences
  • Blended Families
  • Infertility
  • Adoption
  • Codependency
  • Anger
  • Aggression
  • Mixed Race Relationships

Family Counseling

Family counseling provides family members with a safe and secure setting to reduce stress and conflict through the improvement of interactions between family members.

Our family counseling services help with:

  • Parent-child relationships
  • Sibling conflicts
  • Family communication
  • Blended family integration
  • Family transitions (divorce, relocation, etc.)
  • Family dynamics affected by illness or loss
