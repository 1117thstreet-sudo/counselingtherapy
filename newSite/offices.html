Virtual Therapy Options

Telehealth Sessions

We understand that in-person therapy isn't always possible or convenient. That's why we offer secure, HIPAA-compliant video sessions that allow you to connect with your therapist from the comfort of your own space. Virtual therapy provides the same quality care as in-person sessions, with added flexibility.

Our telehealth platform is easy to use and requires only a computer or smartphone with internet access. Your therapist will provide simple instructions for joining your session.

Benefits of Virtual Therapy

  • Convenience and flexibility in scheduling
  • No commute or travel time
  • Access to care from anywhere
  • Comfort of your familiar environment
  • Same evidence-based therapeutic approaches
  • Consistent care even when traveling

What to Expect on Your First Visit

1. Check-In

When you arrive, you'll be greeted by our receptionist who will help you complete any necessary paperwork. We recommend arriving 10-15 minutes early for your first appointment.

2. Waiting Area

Relax in our comfortable waiting area until your therapist is ready to meet you. Complimentary beverages are available for your convenience.

3. Session Space

Your therapist will guide you to a private therapy room where your session will take place. All our therapy rooms are designed for comfort and confidentiality.

4. Initial Consultation

Your first session will focus on getting to know you and understanding your concerns. This is also a time for you to ask questions and determine if the therapist is a good fit for your needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is parking available at your offices?

Our Los Angeles office offers free on-site parking for clients. Our New York office is located near several parking garages, though we recommend using public transportation when possible due to limited parking in Manhattan.

Are your offices accessible for people with disabilities?

Yes, both of our locations are fully wheelchair accessible with elevator access, accessible restrooms, and accommodations for service animals. If you have specific accessibility needs, please let us know when scheduling your appointment so we can ensure a comfortable experience.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Yes, we understand that schedules change. We ask that you provide at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling to avoid a cancellation fee. You can make changes to your appointment by calling our office or using our online portal.

How early should I arrive for my first appointment?

We recommend arriving 10-15 minutes before your scheduled appointment time for your first visit. This allows time to complete any necessary paperwork and get comfortable in our space before your session begins.
