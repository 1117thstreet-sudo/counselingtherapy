Virtual Therapy Options

Can't Visit In Person?

We understand that in-person visits aren't always possible. That's why we offer secure, HIPAA-compliant telehealth sessions, allowing you to connect with your therapist from anywhere.

Our virtual therapy platform provides the same quality care with added convenience and flexibility. All you need is a computer or smartphone with internet access.

Benefits of Virtual Sessions

  • ✓ Convenience and flexibility in scheduling
  • ✓ No commute or travel time
  • ✓ Therapy from the comfort of your home
  • ✓ Same evidence-based therapeutic approaches

Your First Visit

We've designed our intake process to be straightforward and comfortable, allowing us to focus on what matters most: your well-being.

Before Your Appointment

Complete your intake forms online through our secure client portal. This saves time during your first visit and allows your therapist to prepare for your session.

Arrival

Arrive 10-15 minutes early to get settled. You'll be greeted by our receptionist who will help you check in and answer any questions you might have.

Your Session

Your therapist will guide you to a private room where you'll discuss your goals for therapy and begin developing a personalized treatment plan.
