Mission:

To provide the highest quality treatment with integrity and a commitment to respect each person as a unique individual; to honor the power of the mind, body and spirit and to encourage this integration and personal growth through the process of creative self expression and heightened self-awareness.

I am a Licensed Clinical Social Worker with over 25 years experience providing counseling to adults, children / adolescents and families. I am also a trained and certified Gestalt Therapist.

My many years of working in hospital psychiatric emergency rooms, adult and child psychiatric inpatient units and outpatient clinics, social service agencies, homeless shelters for families (couples and children) and public schools (elementary / High School) has afforded me considerable experience with adults, children and adolescents.
